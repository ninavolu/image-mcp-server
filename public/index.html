Setup

2 steps

Add this to your claude_desktop_config.json file:

claude_desktop_config.json JSON
{
  "mcpServers": {
    "image-library": {
      "url": "https://image-mcp-server-production.up.railway.app/sse"
    }
  }
}

When you first connect, you'll be asked to sign in with your email or Google account. That's it.

Privacy Policy

Last updated May 2026

Data we collect

We collect your email address and name when you authenticate via Clerk. No other personal data is collected.

How we use it

Your account information is used solely to authenticate you to the MCP server. No marketing, no analytics, no tracking.

Storage

Authentication is handled by Clerk. Image metadata and files are stored in Supabase. No personal data enters our image database.

Retention & contact

Your data is retained while your account is active. Request deletion anytime at ninavolu7@gmail.com.
